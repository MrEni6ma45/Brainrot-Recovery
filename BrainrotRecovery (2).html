What is Brainrot?

Brain Rot: A Comprehensive Study of Cognitive Disintegration in the Modern Era

Brain rot is a widespread condition affecting an alarming number of internet users today. Characterized by a profound inability to focus, bouts of forgetfulness, and a strange compulsion to scroll endlessly through shitposts and lobotomy memes, this condition is often dismissed as mere laziness. However, a growing body of evidence suggests that brain rot deserves its place in the DSM (Diagnostic and Satirical Manual).

Brainrot Recovery - Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Stop Using Reels or TikTok

Limiting exposure to short-form, mind-numbing content can prevent further cognitive deterioration. Cold turkey, if possible!

Step 2: Talk to Non-Brain Rot-Inflicted Friends

Meaningful conversation with unaffected friends can remind you what real interaction feels like. Seek out in-depth discussions.

Step 3: Touch Grass

Exposure to nature provides a natural mental reset. Go outside, breathe fresh air, and rediscover the world beyond screens.

Step 4: Spend One Hour Outside Each Day

Regular time outside can improve clarity and reduce screen addiction. Even a daily walk can work wonders!
